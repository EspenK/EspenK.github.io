Each of the four Empire factions have a large amount of ships in all sizes and variations.

There are different tech levels. Tech I, or T1, is the default type. These ships are usually set up in a way that allows the player to choose how to use it, as the slot layout and traits are pretty general. Tech II, or T2, are more specialised and their slot layout and traits heavily suggest you use them in a specific way. T2 ships are generally stronger, but only in their role and can often have trouble adapting like a T1 ship could. There are also some ships made by pirate factions. Those combine traits two empire factions, usually making them better then their empire counterparts. Faction ships are considered T1 even thought they are usually stronger.

There are also different many different sizes, divided up in classes. A doctrine is a collection of ship types, each with their designated role.

Class Size Description
Frigate Small Frigates are small, usually fast, ships. They can have multiple roles; scout, tackle, damage, logistics. Outside of a doctrine that consist of only frigate hulls, scout and tackle are the primary tasks for these ships. Frigates have a lot of T2 variants for special roles.
Destroyer Small Destroyers are small but relatively slow, but they deal a lot of damage. They are usually used to deal damage in smaller fleets. The T2 destroyers have some special roles that will be covered later.
Cruiser Medium The most popular class of ships for a long time. It gives a broad range of options for weapon systems, tank and speed. It also provides logistics ships, T2 specialised "healing" ships, wich are used in almost all doctrines with ships of the class cruiser or larger.
Battlecruiser Medium A battlecruiser is larger and slower then a cruiser. It has a similar roles to cruisers as a mainline ship. It has more damage and tank.
Battleship Large Big boys fly these.
Capital X-Large Cool kids fly these.
Supercapitals X-Large Real men fly these.
